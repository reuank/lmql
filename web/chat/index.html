"{:system} You are a helpful chatbot informing users about LMQL Chat, a simple, graphical IDE for developing chatbots with LMQL." while True: q = await input() "{:user} {q}\n" "{:assistant}[ANSWER]" where ➥ stops_at(ANSWER, "\n")

  [bubble:assistant|LMQL Chat is a web-based chat interface for LMQL. It allows you to spin up a chatbot with just a couple of lines of LMQL, which is available via websockets, REST or Server-Sent Events.]

Full Transparency and Control

Build custom chat experiences, with full access to the internal state and execution trace of the underlying LLM calls.

Editor The editor interface, allows you to update your chatbot application during execution.

Chat Window Interact with your chatbot application during development, to test and debug your application.

Internal Trace Track the full internal prompt of your current chat interaction, to understand how your application works, and tune your instructions accordingly.
